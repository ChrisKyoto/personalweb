Arteased

Project Background

Arteased is a fine art marketplace, display and market fine art (physical gallery-type arts), such as painting, drawing, sculpture, etc. Arteased is a startup with self-funded.

Problem

Emerging artists having problem being a full time artist cause by difficulty finding their marke. Building artists career are strongly correlated with credibility, and exposure in art ecosystem. Arteased is determine to solve other industry problem with innovative products, such as crowdfunding for artist projects, implement smartcontracts for legimation, etc.

Solution

Build marketplace and community for marketing fine art focusing on emerging artist with low fee, intuitive design, and innovative product.
